Department Overview

📚 About Department

The General Department serves as the backbone of technical education, providing foundational knowledge in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, English, and other essential subjects. We prepare students with the core competencies required for engineering excellence.

🎯 Our Role

  • First year teaching for all branches
  • Foundation courses in sciences
  • Mathematics & statistics
  • English communication skills
  • Environmental studies
  • Physics
  • Basic engineering drawing

💡 Focus Areas

  • Strong conceptual understanding
  • Practical applications
  • Problem-solving skills
  • Communication proficiency
  • Analytical thinking
  • Holistic development

Vision & Mission

🔭 Vision

Laboratories & Facilities

🔬 Physics Laboratory

Well-equipped lab with modern instruments for experiments in mechanics, optics, electricity, and modern physics. Students perform hands-on experiments to understand theoretical concepts.

🧪 Chemistry Laboratory

Fully equipped chemistry lab for analytical and physical chemistry experiments. Includes facilities for titrations, qualitative analysis, and material testing.

💻 Computer Lab

Modern computer lab with latest software for CAD, programming, MS Excel. Students learn AutoCAD, and other engineering softwares.

📚 Library Resources

Extensive collection of reference books, journals, and digital resources for mathematics, physics, chemistry, and communication skills development.

🎙️ Language Lab

Dedicated language lab with audio-visual aids for enhancing English communication skills, pronunciation, and listening comprehension.

✏️ Drawing Halls

Spacious drawing hall with individual drawing boards, instruments, and CAD workstations for learning technical drawing and design fundamentals.
