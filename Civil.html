Building tomorrow's infrastructure through sustainable practices, innovative design, and excellence in structural, environmental, and urban engineering

Department Overview

📚 About Department

The Civil Engineering department focuses on planning, designing, and constructing infrastructure projects. We train students in structural design, transportation, water resources, environmental engineering, and sustainable construction practices.

🎯 Program Details

  • Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)
  • Intake: 60 Students per year
  • Affiliation: SBTET, Telangana
  • Approval: AICTE Approved

💼 Career Opportunities

  • Civil Engineer
  • Structural Engineer
  • Site Engineer
  • Project Manager
  • Quantity Surveyor
  • Construction Manager
  • Urban Planner

Vision & Mission

🔭 Vision

To be a premier department in civil engineering education, producing competent professionals who contribute to sustainable infrastructure development and societal progress through innovative and ethical practices.

🎯 Mission

  • Provide quality civil engineering education
  • Focus on sustainable construction
  • Industry-oriented practical training
  • Promote research and innovation
  • Develop socially responsible engineers
