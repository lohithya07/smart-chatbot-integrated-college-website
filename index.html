Our Legacy

Government Polytechnic for Women, Siddipet is a premier technical institution ranked among the top 5 women polytechnics in Telangana. We are committed to empowering women through quality education and fostering a spirit of innovation and excellence.

With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a commitment to holistic development, we prepare our students to excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Vision

To nurture technically competent and socially responsible women engineers who can compete globally while maintaining strong ethical values and contributing to sustainable development. We envision a future where our graduates are leaders in innovation, entrepreneurship, and positive social change.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide quality technical education fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership among women students. We are committed to creating an inclusive learning environment that encourages critical thinking, practical skills development, and lifelong learning.
