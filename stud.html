📊 Check Your Results - SBTET Portal

Access official examination results

🎓 Official SBTET Results Portal

Check your examination results directly from the State Board of Technical Education and Training (SBTET), Telangana official website

Go to SBTET Results Portal 🔗

📌 How to Check Your Results:

  1. Click on "Go to SBTET Results Portal" button above

  • Original Certificates: Collect from college office after announcement

📊 Official SBTET Attendance Portal

Check your attendance records and ensure you meet the minimum attendance requirement (75%) for examination eligibility

Go to SBTET Attendance Portal 🔗

⚠️ Attendance Requirement Alert:

  • Minimum Required: 75% attendance is mandatory to appear for examinations
  • Condonation: Available only in exceptional cases with valid medical certificates
  • Track Regularly: Monitor your attendance weekly to avoid last-minute issues
  • Action Required: If attendance falls below 75%, contact your class teacher immediately

  1. Click on "Go to SBTET Attendance Portal" button above

  4. Enter your PIN (Hall Ticket Number) or Roll Number
  5. Select the current semester
  6. Click on "View Attendance" or "Submit" button
  7. Your attendance details will be displayed subject-wise

🎫 Download Hall Tickets - SBTET Portal

Complete feedback to download hall tickets

⚠️

Feedback Submission Mandatory!

As per SBTET regulations, you must complete the student feedback before downloading your hall ticket. Feedback helps improve teaching quality and curriculum.

🎫 Official SBTET Hall Ticket Portal

After completing feedback, download your examination hall tickets from the official SBTET portal. Hall tickets are usually available 7-10 days before examinations.

Go to SBTET Hall Ticket Portal 🔗

📋 Prerequisites for Hall Ticket Download:

  1. ✅ Complete Student Feedback - Mandatory for all subjects
  2. ✅ Minimum 75% Attendance - Check your attendance status
  3. ✅ Fee Payment - All fees must be cleared
  4. ✅ Library Clearance - No pending books
  5. ✅ Examination Registration - Must be completed before deadline

📌 How to Download Hall Ticket:

  1. Step 1: Complete feedback submission (mandatory)
  2. Step 2: Visit SBTET Hall Ticket Portal using button above
  3. Step 3: Select your examination scheme (e.g., C-16, C-18, etc.)
  4. Step 4: Enter your PIN (Hall Ticket Number)
  5. Step 5: Enter the verification code displayed
  6. Step 6: Click on "Get Hall Ticket" button
  7. Step 7: Download and save the PDF file
  8. Step 8: Take multiple printouts (at least 2 copies)

⚠️ Important Instructions:

  • Mandatory Document: Hall ticket is mandatory for examination entry
  • Feedback Completion: Cannot download without completing feedback
  • Verification: Check all details carefully (Name, DOB, Photo, Subjects)
  • Corrections: Report any errors to college office immediately
  • Photo: Paste your recent passport-size photo if required
  • Preservation: Keep hall ticket safe throughout examination period
  • Each Day: Bring hall ticket for every examination

✅ Details to Verify on Hall Ticket:

  • Full Name (as per college records)
  • Date of Birth
  • Father's/Mother's Name
  • PIN (Hall Ticket Number)
  • College Code: 520 (GPWS Siddipet)
  • Examination Center Details
  • List of Subjects
  • Examination Dates and Timings
  • Photograph (if pre-printed)

⭐ Student Feedback - SBTET Portal

Your feedback matters - Help improve education quality

📝 Official SBTET Feedback Portal

Submit your honest feedback about faculty teaching methods, course content, and facilities. Your feedback is confidential and helps improve the quality of education.

Go to SBTET Feedback Portal 🔗

🚨 Mandatory Requirement:

As per SBTET guidelines, feedback submission is mandatory before downloading hall tickets. Students who do not complete feedback will not be able to access their hall tickets.

⚠️ No Feedback = No Hall Ticket

📌 How to Submit Feedback:

  1. Click on "Go to SBTET Feedback Portal" button above
  2. Navigate to the Student Feedback section
  3. Enter your PIN (Hall Ticket Number)
  4. Select your college: 520 - GPWS Siddipet
  5. Choose your department and semester
  6. Rate each faculty member and subject (1-5 stars)
  7. Provide honest and constructive feedback
  8. Submit the feedback form
  9. Save/Print the confirmation receipt
👨‍🏫

Faculty Feedback

Rate teaching methods, communication skills, subject knowledge, and approachability of each faculty member.

📚

Course Content

Evaluate course curriculum, relevance, difficulty level, and practical applicability of subjects.

🏫

Infrastructure

Share feedback on lab facilities, library, classrooms, and other college amenities.

💡 Feedback Guidelines:

  • Be Honest: Your genuine feedback helps improve education quality
  • Be Constructive: Provide specific suggestions for improvement
  • Be Respectful: Maintain professional language throughout
  • Be Complete: Fill all sections - incomplete feedback won't be accepted
  • Be Timely: Submit before the deadline (usually 1 week before exams)
  • Confidential: Your identity is kept anonymous

✅ After Submitting Feedback:

  1. You will receive a confirmation message on screen
  2. Save or take a screenshot of the confirmation
  3. You can now proceed to download your hall ticket
  4. Feedback submission is one-time per semester
  5. Cannot modify feedback after submission
