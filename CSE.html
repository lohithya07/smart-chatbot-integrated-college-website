Department Overview

📚 About Department

The Computer Science & Engineering department was established in 2014 with a vision to create skilled professionals capable of meeting the dynamic demands of the IT industry. We focus on practical learning, industry exposure, and holistic development.

🎯 Program Details

  • Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)
  • Intake: 60+ Students per year
  • Affiliation: SBTET, Telangana
  • Approval: AICTE Approved
