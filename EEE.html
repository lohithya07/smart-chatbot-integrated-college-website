Department Overview

📚 About Department

The Electrical & Electronics Engineering department focuses on power generation, transmission, distribution, and renewable energy technologies. We prepare students to tackle modern energy challenges with sustainable solutions.

🎯 Program Details

  • Duration: 3 Years (6 Semesters)
  • Intake: 60 Students per year
  • Affiliation: SBTET, Telangana
  • Approval: AICTE Approved

💼 Career Opportunities

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Power Systems Engineer
  • Control Systems Engineer
  • Renewable Energy Specialist
  • Instrumentation Engineer
  • Maintenance Engineer

Vision & Mission
